renovation plan

Renovating is the “new in thing”, everyone’s doing it! Almost everyone fantasizes about changing one or more things about their home. We offer a designer’s eye to provide you with a tailored shopping list to achieve the home of your dreams. Perfect for the DIY renovators that need a bit of extra guidance.

residential building design

Many people have a pretty good idea of what their dream home looks and feels like, but have little to no idea how to arrive at the desired outcome. We are architectural designers, and by combining our skills and expertise with your needs and desires, we will deliver a house you can call home, one that you’ll love to live in.

floor plan review

Buying “off-plan” is quite common these days, however, these plans don’t take specific site conditions into consideration. We’ll go through the plans with you and take note of any changes you may want as well as provide suggestions based on your property. Building a new home is a rare opportunity and something you’re likely to only do once, if at all, so let us help you make sure you do it right the first time round.

spatial planning

Whether it’s an indoor or outdoor space, a whole house or a single room, we are here to help if you need a hand with furniture arrangementto optimise your space and ensure it suits your needs. This is done with our 3D software to give you an accurate idea of the end result.
