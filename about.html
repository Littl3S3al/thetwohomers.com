who we are

The Two Homers is a Sydney-based design duo, their home renovation and design consultancy business was founded by Jess and Dave Homer in 2018.

Jess is a highly organised architectural designer and self-motivated creative enthusiast, with over 5 years of international experience specialising in designing once in a lifetime experiences for guests in 5-star exclusive boutique hotels. Paired with the unique skill to recognise the true potential of underutilised space, her vision and excitement are brought to life through her designs.

Dave is a strong, passionate designer with over 5 years of experience leading a range of residential and hospitality projects for award-winning architecture firms both locally and internationally. Specialising in medium and high-end residential projects, he is well versed in everything renovation related. He prides himself on attention to detail and spends his free time designing beautiful custom once-off furniture.

our ethos

Drawing from our youth, growing up in the bushveld of South Africa we feel an intimate connection with nature. The vast array of complimentary tones, textures, and materiality culminating in perfect harmony can be simply breathtaking. We try embrace this composition throughout each of our designs.

Simplicity and Balance lead to a minimalist approach. We often see the shortfall in many inexperienced designers/DIY-ers by over complicating and cluttering their canvas. We try to be as efficient as possible, a light well placed intervention often achieves a more timeless composition.

Embracing a full array of the sensory experience whilst contemplating a space will always lead to a more comfortable sense of being. We consider the impact of each element within a design whether it be light, sound, smell, warmth, texture or airflow, every component has its own innate properties and plays a roll in composing a harmonic symphony of space.
