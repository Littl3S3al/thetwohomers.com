our brand

Generally, people are under the impression that good home design is expensive and that hiring a designer will use up a large chunk of their budget, so they try and do everything themselves, wasting a lot of time and often money too.

We’ve curated various design packages to suit a range of renovation or interior design projects, from small DIY renos to bespoke design, we have an offering for every budget and have made our pricing publicly available.

Giving our clients the peace of mind that their personalised dream renovation or interior makeover will suit their budget and at the same time, be an enjoyable process.

our services

renovation plan

Renovating is the “new in thing”, everyone’s doing it! Almost everyone fantasizes about changing one or more things about their home. We offer a designer’s eye to provide you with a tailored shopping list to achieve the home of your dreams. Perfect for the DIY renovators that need a bit of extra guidance.

residential building design

Many people have a pretty good idea of what their dream home looks and feels like, but have little to no idea how to arrive at the desired outcome. We are architectural designers, and by combining our skills and expertise with your needs and desires, we will deliver a house you can call home, one that you’ll love to live in.

finishes, fixtures + fittings selection

The cherry on top, taking your design to the next level. From paint colours, floor finishes, feature tiles and ceilings, to tapware, hardware, appliances and fittings, this layer of design is where the wow factor comes from. This is the fun part where everything comes together and your vision comes alive.

lighting layout, design + selection

Lighting plays a huge role in the completion of any space, it’s important to remember that it’s not only about what it looks like, but also how it can affect our moods. Once we’re done, your home’s lighting will not only look stunning, it will make you feel amazing too!
