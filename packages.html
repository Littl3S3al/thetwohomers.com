#1
package 1

consultation

This is the first step for all of our packages, the starting point of your design project, we usually start off with an email and phone call briefing before moving on to a face to face meeting.

1
hour
from $300
1-2 weeks

A face-to-face meeting in your home (or on a video platform) that usually lasts about an hour and allows us to have an in-depth conversation about your project requirements. We’ll discuss ideas that you may already have so that we can get a better sense of your personal style, specific requirements and project time frames.

We’ll take some photos and measurements of the spaces that require work, ultimately, we’ll use this time to gather all of the information required to move forward into the concept phase of your project.

You’ll receive a detailed summary of the design meeting as well as a brief for the next steps going forward.
