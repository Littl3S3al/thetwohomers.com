our brand

Generally, people are under the impression that good home design is expensive and that hiring a designer will use up a large chunk of their budget, so they try and do everything themselves, wasting a lot of time and often money too.

We’ve curated various design packages to suit a range of renovation or interior design projects, from small DIY renos to bespoke design, we have an offering for every budget and have made our pricing publicly available.

Giving our clients the peace of mind that their personalised dream renovation or interior makeover will suit their budget and at the same time, be an enjoyable process.

Be faithful to your own taste, because nothing you really like is ever out of style.

– Billy Baldwin
